1 /*
2  This program is the receiver for nRF24_Send_to_RPi that was originally written
3  for the Raspberry Pi and ported back to UNO for completeness.
4 
5  Found out that I cannot have two startListening() statement, one in setup() and another
6  inside the loop() and the radio.available() will not received anything at all.
7 
8  The receiver will accept 6 pipes and display received payload to the screen
9 
10  The receiver will return the receive payload to the sender usinbg the same pipe
11  to calculate the rtt if the payload matched
12 
13  Max payload size is 32 bytes
